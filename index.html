1. Overview

Activity Launcher & Shortcuts is an Android utility app that helps users view available app activities, open supported screens, and create shortcuts for faster access.

The app itself does not sell personal data. Some information may be processed by third-party services that support app functionality, such as Google AdMob for advertising and Google Firebase for analytics, diagnostics, and performance monitoring.

The app is designed to process core functionality on-device. However, third-party SDKs included in the app may automatically collect certain device, usage, and diagnostic information as described below.

2. Information We Access

The app may access or process the following categories of information:

  • Installed app information on your device, such as app names, package names, icons, and available activities, in order to display launchable screens and create shortcuts.
  • Device and technical information, such as device model, operating system version, app version, language, country, IP-derived general region, crash logs, and performance diagnostics.
  • Usage information, such as screens viewed, interactions inside the app, session data, and feature usage events.
  • Advertising-related data, which may include identifiers and signals used by ad providers to serve, measure, and improve ads, subject to your device settings and applicable law.

The exact information collected by third-party services may vary depending on your device, Android version, region, consent choices, and SDK behavior.

3. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

  • Provide the core functionality of the app, including browsing activities and creating shortcuts.
  • Maintain, debug, and improve app stability, usability, and performance.
  • Measure feature usage and understand how users interact with the app.
  • Display advertisements and evaluate their effectiveness.
  • Prevent abuse, errors, fraud, and technical issues.

4. Advertising

This app may display ads using Google AdMob or related Google advertising services. Advertising partners may collect certain information from your device or app interactions to provide, personalize, measure, and improve ads, subject to applicable settings and consent requirements.

Ad-related processing may include the use of identifiers, approximate location derived from IP address, device information, and ad performance data.

You can manage certain advertising preferences through your device settings and, where applicable, through consent prompts shown in the app.

5. Analytics & Firebase

This app may use Google Firebase, including services such as Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics.

  • Firebase Analytics may collect usage events, engagement data, device details, and app interaction information.
  • Firebase Crashlytics may collect crash traces, device state information, and technical diagnostics to help identify and fix bugs.

These services may process information on Google servers in accordance with Google’s privacy practices.

More information can be found in Google’s privacy documentation: Google Privacy Policy.

6. Data Sharing

We do not directly sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers only as necessary to operate, analyze, secure, and monetize the app, including:

  • Google AdMob and related ad technology providers
  • Google Firebase and related Google infrastructure services
  • Legal authorities if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security

7. Data Retention

On-device data used for core functionality is generally handled locally. Data collected by third-party SDKs may be retained according to those providers’ own retention policies and operational needs.

We keep only the information reasonably necessary to provide and improve the app, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes or technical issues.

8. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect information related to this app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

9. Children’s Privacy
